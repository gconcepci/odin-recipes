Lasagna

What is Lasagna?

Lasagna is Garfield's favorite dish. I just had some lasagna today actually. Its delicious. Its like a layer cake but with pasta, cheese, and meat. Let's make some lasagna!

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground beef
  • 9 Lasagna sheets
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Cook beef in the pan.
  2. Boil lasagna sheets.
  3. Place lasagna sheets in pan then add meat, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Layer by doing that again.
  5. Put in oven.
