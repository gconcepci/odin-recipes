Chicken Parm

What is Chicken Parm?

Chicken Parm is an Italian-American comfort food staple. This simple recipe has a crispy breading made from panko breadcrumbs and grated parmesan cheese, just enough marinara sauce on top, and melted mozzarella cheese.

The recipe will give you the confidence to replicate it in your own home.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Garlic powder
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Panko
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning
  • Olive oil
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Cut chicken breasts thinly and dip into egg and then panko mixture.
  2. Fry chicken in olive oil.
  3. Top with sauce and cheese.
  4. Finish in oven for cheese to melt.
