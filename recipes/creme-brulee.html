Creme Brulee

What is Creme Brulee?

Creme Brulee is the world's fanciest dessert. It is a vanilla egg custard that is slowly baked and then topped with a caramelized glass-like top shell. The best part is breaking this top with a spoon like Zumbo. Tastes good too. My wife loves this stuff.

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream
  • Vanilla bean
  • Salt
  • Egg yolks
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Combine cream, vanilla bean, and salt and cook on low heat.
  2. Beat yolks and sugar. Pour into cream and stir.
  3. Bake in ramekins for 30 minutes surrounded with water.
  4. Top with sugar and put a flame over the top until sugar browns.
